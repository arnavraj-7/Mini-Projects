About BMI
BMI (Body Mass Index) is a numerical value calculated from a person's weight and height. It is used as a general indicator of body fat and helps assess whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. However, it does not measure body fat directly and may not be accurate for athletes or individuals with high muscle mass.
